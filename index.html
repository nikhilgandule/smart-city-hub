Turkish Airlines Flight Makes Emergency Landing

A flight from Kathmandu to Istanbul made an emergency landing in Kolkata after a possible engine issue.

Flight Emergency Report:

Flight TK-504 from Kathmandu (KTM) to Istanbul (IST) declared an emergency due to a suspected engine issue and made a safe emergency landing at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata. All 284 passengers and crew members were evacuated safely. The aircraft is being inspected by technical teams.
